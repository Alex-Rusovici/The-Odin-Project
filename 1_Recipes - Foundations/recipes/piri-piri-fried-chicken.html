Piri Piri Fried Chicken

Description

Piri piri fried chicken is a twist on classic buttermilk fried chicken that incorporates popular African and Portuguese piri piri seasonings, such as birdâ€™s eye chili peppers, paprika, salt, lemon peels, oregano, and garlic, to create citrusy, garlicky, and herbal notes.

I love exploring different spices in order to come up with new and exciting family meals. For me, this happens through travel (overseas), but it also happens at my local spice stores. During a "trip" one afternoon, I stumbled across a piri piri spice blend, did some research on the spice, and the rest was history! As my creative juices started flowing, I came up with this piri piri fried chicken.

This fried chicken recipe is perfect for family dinners, weeknight meals, or picnics. Serve with a side of greens and coleslaw and dig in!

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 2 1/2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon piri piri spice blend
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 3 1/2 pound bone-in skin-on chicken legs, thighs and wings

For the flour coating:

  • 2 cups (272g) all-purpos flour
  • 2 tablespoons piri piri spice blend
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon kisher salt

For frying chicken:

  • Peanut oil or canola oil

Method of cooking

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Make the flour coating for chicken
  3. Dredge the chicken pieces in flour mixture
  4. Let the floured chicken rest
  5. Heat the frying oil
  6. Fry the chicken
  7. Place chicken on a rack to drain excess oil
  8. Serve
